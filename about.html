ABOUT

Teresa is passionate about crocheting.Her first contact with a yarn was as a child when she learned the craft from her grandmother. Many years later, she rediscovered her skills as a joyful hobby and as a productive business.

She likes to experiment with different types of styles using a wide variety of hook sizes and types of yarn. Teresa also devotes much of her time to sharing her knowledge and passion for crochet.

Teresa has two very naughty dogs and a loving husband who insists he doesn't care that their house has turned into a yarn warehouse. She currently lives in Toronto and studies computer programming (the reason behind this decision remains a mystery).
